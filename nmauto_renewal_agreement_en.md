# Auto-renewal Agreement

**Effective Date: January 1, 2025**

## 1. Auto-renewal Description

### 1.1 Auto-renewal Mechanism

Drudge's Diary membership subscriptions use an auto-renewal mechanism. After you subscribe to the membership service, the system will automatically renew your subscription at the end of each subscription period to ensure you can continue to enjoy membership benefits.

### 1.2 Renewal Period

- **Monthly Membership**: Automatically renews every 30 days
- **Yearly Membership**: Automatically renews every 365 days

Renewal will occur within 24 hours before the end of the current subscription period.

## 2. Renewal Period and Pricing

### 2.1 Monthly Membership (Product ID: com.cpcamera.nmrj.monthlyL)

- **Renewal Period**: Every 30 days
- **Renewal Price**: ¥8/month
- **Renewal Method**: Automatically charged from the payment method associated with your Apple ID

### 2.2 Yearly Membership (Product ID: com.cpcamera.nmrj.yearly)

- **Renewal Period**: Every 365 days
- **Renewal Price**: ¥88/year
- **Renewal Method**: Automatically charged from the payment method associated with your Apple ID

### 2.3 Price Consistency

Renewal prices are the same as the initial purchase price. If we adjust prices before renewal, we will notify you in advance. Price adjustments will take effect in the next renewal period.

## 3. How to Cancel Auto-renewal

### 3.1 Cancellation Time Requirement

You can cancel auto-renewal **at least 24 hours** before the end of the subscription period. After cancellation, you can still use the current subscription period normally, and it will not automatically renew after expiration.

### 3.2 Cancellation Steps (Detailed Instructions)

Please follow these steps to cancel auto-renewal:

1. Open the "Settings" app on your iPhone or iPad
2. Tap [Your Name] (Apple ID) at the top
3. Select "Subscriptions"
4. Find "Drudge's Diary" in the subscription list
5. Tap "Drudge's Diary"
6. Tap the "Cancel Subscription" button
7. Confirm the cancellation

### 3.3 Impact After Cancellation

- **Current Subscription Period**: After cancellation, you can still use all membership features during the current subscription period
- **After Expiration**: After the subscription expires, membership features will automatically stop, and you will no longer be able to use AI features
- **Resubscribe**: You can resubscribe at any time to restore membership benefits

### 3.4 Cancellation Confirmation

After completing the cancellation, you will receive a confirmation email from Apple. You can also check the cancellation status in "Settings" > "Subscriptions".

## 5. Renewal Notifications

### 5.1 Pre-renewal Notification

Apple will notify you before renewal through the following methods:

- **Email Notification**: Sent to the email associated with your Apple ID
- **App Store Notification**: Displays renewal reminders in the App Store

### 5.2 Viewing Renewal Information

You can view renewal information through the following methods:

1. Open "Settings" > [Your Name] > "Subscriptions"
2. Select "Drudge's Diary"
3. View the next renewal date and price

### 5.3 Renewal Failure Handling

If renewal fails (such as insufficient balance in the payment method), Apple will:

- Send a notification reminding you to update your payment method
- Automatically retry renewal after the payment method is updated
- Automatically cancel the subscription if renewal fails multiple times

## 6. Payment Method Management

### 6.1 Updating Payment Method

You can update your payment method in Apple ID settings:

1. Open "Settings" > [Your Name] > "Payment & Shipping"
2. Select "Add Payment Method" or "Edit" existing payment method
3. Complete the payment method update

### 6.2 Payment Method Requirements

- Payment method must be valid and have sufficient balance
- Supports credit cards, debit cards, Apple Pay, etc.
- Please refer to Apple's documentation for specific supported payment methods

## 7. Frequently Asked Questions

### 7.1 How to confirm if auto-renewal is enabled?

In "Settings" > "Subscriptions", if "Drudge's Diary" shows "Enabled", it means auto-renewal is enabled.

### 7.2 Can I still use membership features after cancellation?

Yes. After canceling auto-renewal, you can still use all membership features during the current subscription period until the subscription expires.

### 7.3 How to re-enable auto-renewal?

Resubscribe to the membership service to automatically enable auto-renewal.

### 7.4 Will renewal prices change?

Renewal prices are the same as the initial purchase price. If prices are adjusted, we will notify you in advance.
